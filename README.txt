### HelloMap ###

===========================================================================
DESCRIPTION:

Hello World Sample App displays a simple NMAMapView on screen. The project includes all
dependencies required by NMAKit.framework.

===========================================================================
INSTRUCTIONS:

1) Copy NMAKit.framework in a “framework” subfolder at the same
level as the application.
2) Change the app bundle identifier from "com.yourcompany.HelloMap" to the
bundle identifier of your app.
3) Update AppDelegate with the app id, and app code assigned to you by your HERE representative.

===========================================================================
BUILD REQUIREMENTS:

* Xcode 7
* iOS 8 SDK or above
* HERE Starter SDK Version 1.0 or above

===========================================================================
TARGET PLATFORM:

* iOS 8.x & iOS 9.x
